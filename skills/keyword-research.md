# Skill: Bengali Keyword Research
# BanglaTools — bengalitools.com
# Use this skill to find new keyword opportunities for tools and blog posts

---

## Purpose

Find low-competition, high-search-volume Bengali and English keywords
for bengalitools.com. Produce a prioritized, actionable content plan
so there is always a clear answer to "what should I build or write next?"

This site is NOT a local service business. It is a bilingual utility
tool site targeting Bengali speakers worldwide. Keywords are evaluated
on search volume and competition — NOT on CPC or lead value.

---

## Site Context — Read Before Every Research Session

- Site: bengalitools.com
- Language: Bengali-first, English toggle on every page
- Audience: Bengali speakers in West Bengal, Bangladesh, UK, USA, Gulf
- Content types: Tool pages + blog posts
- Monetization: Google AdSense + affiliate links
- Domain: New — avoid high-competition keywords until month 6+
- Biggest advantage: Almost no quality Bengali-language tool sites exist

## Step 1 — Understand What Type of Content Is Needed

Before searching for keywords, identify which type is being researched.
These are evaluated completely differently.

### Tool Pages
A dedicated page for one specific calculator or converter.
URL pattern: `/converters/kg-to-lbs` or `/health/bmi-calculator`
Goal: Rank for the Bengali + English name of the tool
Example primary keyword: "বিএমআই ক্যালকুলেটর" or "BMI calculator Bangla"
Evaluation: Search volume + competition difficulty

### Blog Posts
An article that answers a specific question and embeds a relevant tool.
URL pattern: `/blog/bmi-healthy-range-bangla`
Goal: Rank for a specific question Bengali users search
Example primary keyword: "বিএমআই কত হলে স্বাস্থ্যকর"
Evaluation: Search volume + competition difficulty + which tool it supports

## Step 2 — Bengali Keyword Research Process

### 2A — Start With Bengali Search Patterns

Bengali users search in 4 different ways. Research all 4 patterns
for every topic:

**Pattern 1 — Pure Bengali script**
Example: "বিএমআই ক্যালকুলেটর", "ডলার রেট আজকে"
These have the lowest competition. Almost no sites target these.

**Pattern 2 — Bengali question format**
Example: "বিএমআই কত হলে ভালো", "১ কেজি সমান কত পাউন্ড"
High volume, very low competition in Bengali.

**Pattern 3 — English word + Bengali context**
Example: "BMI calculator বাংলা", "dollar rate আজকে"
Growing pattern especially among younger Bengali users.

**Pattern 4 — Pure English**
Example: "BMI calculator", "USD to INR today"
Higher competition but also higher volume. Target these second.

For every tool or blog topic, identify keywords in all 4 patterns.

### 2B — Search Volume Signals (Without Paid Tools)

Estimate search volume using these free signals:

**High Volume Signals:**
- Google autocompletes the query immediately when you start typing
- "People Also Ask" box appears in search results
- Multiple different websites and forums answer this question
- The query appears in Google Trends India or Bangladesh
- Related searches at the bottom of Google show many variations

**Medium Volume Signals:**
- Google autocompletes but with slight delay
- 1-2 "People Also Ask" questions appear
- Some forums and blogs cover this topic

**Low Volume Signals:**
- Google does not autocomplete
- No "People Also Ask" box
- Very few results (under 50,000)
- Only Wikipedia or one major site covers it

Label every keyword: 🔥 High / 📊 Medium / 💧 Low

### 2C — Competition Difficulty Check

For every keyword, check the actual Google search result and look for:

**🟢 Easy — Target immediately (new site can rank):**
- Forum results visible (Reddit, Quora, Bengali forums)
- Results are old (2+ years since last updated)
- Results are thin (short pages, no images, no structure)
- Small unknown websites rank alongside big ones
- No dominant winner — mixed types of sites
- Bengali results appear but look poorly made
- "People Also Ask" present but answers are weak

**🟡 Medium — Target after month 3:**
- Mix of small and medium authority sites
- Some results are well-optimized but not all
- Big directories rank but some smaller sites appear too
- Most results are English, Bengali results are weak

**🔴 Hard — Avoid for first 6 months:**
- Only major brands rank (Google, Wikipedia, major news sites)
- Every result is well-optimized, long, recent, and fast
- No forums, no small sites anywhere on page 1
- All results are from sites with massive authority

## Step 3 — Topic Categories to Research

Always search for keywords across all these categories:

### Category A — Tool Name Keywords
Direct searches for the tool itself.

Search patterns to try:
- "[tool name] বাংলা"
- "[tool name] বাংলায়"
- "[tool name] online"
- "[tool name] calculator"
- "online [tool name]"
- "free [tool name]"

Examples:
- "BMI calculator বাংলা"
- "বিএমআই ক্যালকুলেটর"
- "kg to lbs converter"
- "কেজি থেকে পাউন্ড"

### Category B — Question Keywords (Blog Post Targets)
Specific questions Bengali users type into Google.

Search patterns to try:
- "১ [unit] সমান কত [unit]"
- "[topic] কত হলে ভালো"
- "[topic] কীভাবে বের করব"
- "[topic] কত হওয়া উচিত"
- "[food item] এ কত ক্যালরি"
- "আজকের [currency] রেট"
- "[topic] বাংলায় হিসাব"

Examples:
- "১ কেজি সমান কত পাউন্ড"
- "বিএমআই কত হলে স্বাস্থ্যকর"
- "রসগোল্লায় কত ক্যালরি"
- "আজকের ডলারের রেট কত"

### Category C — Bengali Food Calorie Keywords
Major opportunity — almost no Bengali sites cover this properly.

Search patterns to try:
- "[Bengali food name] এ কত ক্যালরি"
- "[Bengali food name] ক্যালরি"
- "[Bengali food name] calories"
- "বাংলা খাবার ক্যালরি চার্ট"

Priority foods to research:
মাছের ঝোল, ডাল, ভাত, রুটি, লুচি, খিচুড়ি, মিষ্টি দই,
রসগোল্লা, শোরষে ইলিশ, পান্তা ভাত, চিঁড়া, মুড়ি, সিঙারা,
কচুরি, পরোটা, বিরিয়ানি, মোগলাই পরোটা

### Category D — Currency and Rate Keywords
Daily searches — very high frequency from Gulf Bengalis and NRIs.

Search patterns to try:
- "আজকের [currency] রেট"
- "[currency] থেকে টাকা"
- "১ [currency] সমান কত টাকা"
- "[currency] rate আজকে"
- "[currency] to INR today"
- "[currency] to BDT today"

Priority currencies:
USD, AED (Dubai), EUR, GBP, SAR (Saudi Riyal), QAR (Qatar),
KWD (Kuwait), BDT (Bangladesh Taka), SGD (Singapore)

### Category E — India-Specific Keywords
Zero competition in Bengali. These are completely untapped.

Search patterns to try:
- "জিএসটি কীভাবে হিসাব করব"
- "আজকের সোনার দাম [city]"
- "বাংলা তারিখ আজ"
- "বাংলা সন ক্যালকুলেটর"
- "[number] বাংলায় কীভাবে লিখব"
- "মাসিক বেতন থেকে ঘণ্টায় বেতন"

## Step 5 — Competition Analysis for Top Keywords

For the top 10 keywords identified, do a deeper analysis:

For each keyword check:
1. What are the top 3 results on Google?
2. Are any results in Bengali? If yes, how good are they?
3. How long is the content on the top result?
4. When was the top result last updated?
5. Does a "People Also Ask" box appear?
6. What questions appear in the PAA box?
   (These become FAQ questions for your page)
7. What do the related searches at the bottom show?
   (These become secondary keywords and content ideas)

Document findings like this:

**Keyword:** বিএমআই ক্যালকুলেটর
**Top 3 results:** [list them]
**Bengali results:** Yes/No — quality: Good/Poor/None
**Top result length:** ~800 words
**Top result age:** 2023
**PAA questions found:**
  - বিএমআই কত হলে মোটা?
  - মহিলাদের আদর্শ বিএমআই কত?
  - বিএমআই কীভাবে বের করব?
**Related searches:** [list them]
**Verdict:** 🟢 Easy — Bengali results are poor, PAA present, old content ranks

## Quality Rules — Never Break

1. Always check the real Google SERP before rating competition.
   Never guess. Always verify.

2. Search in Bengali script, not just English.
   "বিএমআই ক্যালকুলেটর" and "BMI calculator" have completely
   different competition levels. Research both.

3. Never assign the same primary keyword to two pages.
   This causes cannibalization and hurts all rankings.

4. New site rule: Only recommend 🟢 Easy keywords for the first
   6 months. No 🔴 Hard keywords until the site has authority.

5. Every blog post keyword must support a tool page.
   If a blog topic doesn't naturally link to a tool on the site,
   deprioritize it.

6. Always include the PAA questions found.
   These become the FAQ section on the page and are critical
   for getting the page indexed and featured in search results.

7. Include Bengali script AND English translation for every keyword.
   Claude Code needs both to write bilingual content correctly.
